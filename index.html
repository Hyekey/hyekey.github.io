ABOUT

Hi, I’m Hyekey.

I’m a GeoGuessr content creator from Kerala, India, creating geography-focused videos on YouTube. My content explores the world through map knowledge, regional patterns, and location deduction, with a strong emphasis on thoughtful gameplay and learning through exploration.

I primarily create GeoGuessr videos in Malayalam, making geography and location-based games more accessible and enjoyable for viewers who prefer regional language content. My videos range from competitive gameplay and strategy-driven challenges to calm, exploratory sessions focused on specific regions such as Kerala, India, and other interesting parts of the world.

This website serves as a central hub for my work — a place to showcase featured videos, share my approach to GeoGuessr, and connect with viewers who enjoy geography, maps, and location-based problem solving. I value respectful discussion, curiosity, and genuine interest in geography, and I’m always open to suggestions for future video ideas.

If you enjoy geography-related content, relaxed exploration, or competitive GeoGuessr gameplay, you’re welcome to follow along and explore the world with me.
